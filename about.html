About me

I’m a product designer, mentor, and growth strategist who helps mission-driven teams turn insights into digital experiences that convert, retain, and scale.

I studied PR and advertising (yes, in the film school), then launched my career in boutique marketing and event agencies working on custom campaigns for brands. I wore many hats: copywriter, graphic designer, social media strategist, deck builder, and even event producer.

Eventually the grind wore thin, and I pivoted into UX. These days, I work independently with startups and small businesses, bringing a mix of craft, strategy, and growth thinking to each project.

Outside of work, I’m a certified arts & crafts nerd. You’ll often find me deep in a coloring book. I also take my playlist curation very seriously. If you work with me, expect thoughtful questions, intentional design, and the occasional surprise cameo.
